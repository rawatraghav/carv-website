About Us!

CARV Hindi or Circle of Acting at RV - Hindi, is the hindi exclusive dramatics society of R.V. College of Engineering. Considered one of the best street play and theatre club in the south zone, it is surely requires a multi-level recruiting criteria, but once a part of this club you are assured to have memories of a lifetime.
